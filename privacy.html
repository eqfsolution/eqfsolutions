Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

At EQF Solutions, we collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our services, or contact us for support.

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Service address and billing information
  • Account credentials and security information
  • Communication preferences

Usage Information

  • Internet usage patterns and bandwidth consumption
  • Device information and network performance data
  • Service usage statistics and quality metrics
  • Customer service interaction records

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our internet and cable services
  • Process payments and manage billing
  • Improve our services and network performance
  • Communicate with you about your services and account
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our business
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • In connection with a business transaction or merger
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and employee training
  • Secure network infrastructure and monitoring

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of certain communications
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website and improve our services. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
